Theoretical Condensed Matter Physics

The Theoretical Condensed Matter Physics (CMP) section at IIT Indore focuses on exploring the fundamental properties of materials through advanced theoretical approaches. One faculty member specializes in first-principles simulations using Density Functional Theory (DFT), with research centered on energy storage materials, such as batteries, and the design of innovative materials for sustainable energy applications. Another faculty member works on transport phenomena in low-dimensional systems, investigating quantum materials such as topological insulators and Weyl semimetals to uncover their unique electronic, magnetic, and optical properties. Together, they address a wide range of challenges in theoretical condensed matter physics, from energy solutions to quantum transport.
