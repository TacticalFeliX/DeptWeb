About Us

Physics Department

The Department of Physics at IIT Indore was established in 2009. The department offers a B.Tech program in Engineering Physics, an M.Sc. program in Physics, and a Ph.D. program in Physics. The department has a vibrant research environment with research areas ranging from theoretical and experimental condensed matter physics, quantum information, quantum optics, and quantum computing to high energy physics, astrophysics, and cosmology. The department has a strong focus on interdisciplinary research and collaborations with other departments and institutions. The department has state-of-the-art research facilities and laboratories for both theoretical and experimental research.

Events

Physics Club

The Physics Club at IIT Indore is a student-run organization that aims to promote interest in physics and related fields among students. The club organizes various events, including seminars, workshops, and guest lectures by renowned physicists.

Seminars and Colloquiums

The department regularly organizes seminars and colloquiums where faculty members and invited speakers present their research work. These events provide an excellent opportunity for students to learn about the latest developments in physics.
