Non-linear Dynamics and Complex Networks

The Non-linear Dynamics and Complex Networks, led by Prof. Sarika, is dedicated to advancing the understanding of intricate phenomena in biological, social, and physical systems. This multidisciplinary research group focuses on the interplay between nonlinearity, stochasticity, and emergent behaviors across various domains.
