Experimental Condensed Matter Physics

The Experimental Condensed Matter Physics group at IIT Indore focuses on understanding and innovating the structural, electronic, magnetic, and optical properties of materials. Our research spans nanomaterials, thin films, spectroscopy, and more, pushing the boundaries of material science and its applications in technology.

The groups are highly equipped with the following high-end research equipment (to name a few):

  • Thin Film Deposition Units
  • Terahertz Spectroscopy Instruments
  • X-ray Absorption Fine Structure (XAFS) Setup
  • Nanomaterial Synthesis Labs
  • Surface and Interface Characterization Tools

This section consists of different research groups led by dedicated faculty members specializing in distinct aspects of condensed matter physics and materials science.
